Retro Toy Site

What is the Toy Site Project?

The toy site project is a mock retro site design project for a Graphic Design course at the University of Central Florida. Using 1950's era toy packaging, painted signs, and typography for reference, we are to design and implement a website for a toy company as if we were in the 1950s.

Design Phase

Currently, the project is just out of the design phase. I looked at component layout, branding, color schemes, and textures for the final design. I chose faded red, cyan, and yellow colors after examining common color schemes in 1950s toy packaging. The browns are meant to reference the use of cardboard in the packaging material itself. I contrast this rustic feel in color with italic script like font and a fluidly moving flag for my tagline. I make use of subtle noise textures to mimic the feel of a painted surface as well as flat paper.
