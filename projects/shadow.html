Shadow Fan Site

What is the Shadow Fan Site?

The shadow fan site project is a fan website for a project for a Graphic Design course at the University of Central Florida. Using 1950's era sci-fi icons, comic book art and typography, as well as fan website service and structure, we were to build an unnoficial fan website.

Design Phase

Currently, the project is just out of the design phase. I looked at component layout, branding, color schemes, and textures for the final design. I chose blue, red, and dark grey colors to mimic the color schemes used in the shadow comics. I used vector art in particiular to allow for intense shadowing of the character drawings and illude to comic art. The typography, although not suggestive of the Shadow's character, communicates a sense of comic aesthetic more well known to the general comic book audience.

Development Plans

  1. Use scalable SVG files to make a full responsive experience
  2. Use sliding transitions from one page to the next to simulate reading panels in a comic book
