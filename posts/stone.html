What is the Stone Brewing Email Blast?

Stone Brewing Email Blast

This project was intended to be an excersise in designing around white space and using color only for emphasis or for balance. I picked to use stone brewing as my company of choice because their style branding let me work on a blank slate in terms of aesthetic. The format was meant to be an email blast in order to keep the scope of the project focused on a single unified message.

How I designed the Email Blast

The email blast was designed around communicating one message, "Vertical Epic 12.12.12 has been released". I split this task up into two main objectives.

  1. Highlight the quality of the product
  2. Direct users to find the product

I tackled the first objective by inserting a large photograph of the bottle on the right hand side of the page, pointing up at the header. This forces the user's eye to travel from the bottle back up to the header, creating an association between the product and the brand. I then add a description of the product's taste to complete the product's branding. The second objective is fufilled by including a large padding around a call to action button. This implies importance and insures that the user's eye always lands on it while scanning the page.

My use of color in this project was to mimic the color pallete of the product, and bring life to a monochromatic logo. Because, the logo was not suited for web formats, I designed a colorful header around it. It was designed with the intent to mimic the actual colors of beer. This format also allows for multiple color schemes to be applied to future email blasts.
