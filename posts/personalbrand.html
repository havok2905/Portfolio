Personal Brand

What is my Brand?

First and foremost, the brand I designed is meant to symbolize three main concepts.

  1. Organization
  2. Reliability
  3. A Concept of Near Futurism

I achieve these concepts mainly through my use of color, font, and my personal logo. The blue color is meant to be reminiscent of corporatism, intelligence, and create a calming sensation to the user. This is meant to subliminally communicate reliability which is the common attribute of what I just listed. The white and black colors are used primarily to allow me to insert images and various media seamlessly into the design without much clashing of color. This gives me flexibility as a designer. Also, the white and black colors communicate a sense of cleanliness which is indicative of organization as well as newness due to the illusion of polish.

My choice of fonts are all meant to communicate forward movement. I use slightly italicized fonts to communicate forward movement which is always important in the ever evolving web industry.

My personal logo was designed to communicate a very near and dear value that I hold. The world is ever evolving and changing around us and there is always something new to learn on the horizon. To stay stagnant is to retire and quit. This is why my logo is of a road leading off into the horizon, symbolizing the road to the next new great thing to learn.
