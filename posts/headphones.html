3D Headphones

What was the 3D Headphone Project?

In taking a 3D content creation course in my Junior year in college, I decided to flex the old Autodesk muscles that have been dormant for over a year. The requirement was to model a household object of our choice.

Organic and Mechanical Surfaces

I chose to model my headphones in order to explore mechanical as well as organic shapes in 3D objects. The foam ear pieces are prime examples or organic shape. This contrasts the rigid plastic surfaces which are mechanical in nature. I chose to split the modeling process along these two different categories of shape, and welded them when the time came for smoothing. This allowed me to focus on the organic, mechanical, and transitional portions of the model all separately.
